Sunday Pasta

My most used recipe. Emily and I found this dish in one of those meal prep kits, I believe it was 'HelloFresh'. After a couple minor adjustments it's become a favorite!

Ingredients

  • Pasta, preference rigatoni
  • Ground tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Spinach
  • Ground Turkey/Beef/Pork
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Heat water until boiled, add Pasta, cook to Al Dente
  2. Roughly chop garlic, onion, and carrot
  3. Generously coat a deep frying pan in olive oil, fry until garlic and onion are fragrant
  4. Add ground turkey, season with salt and pepper, cook through
  5. Add Spinach to boiled water for 30 seconds
  6. Add ground tomatoes to pan, pour in balsamic vinegar to taste
  7. Add pasta, top with fresh basil and parmesan
  8. Enjoy!
